Web Site Developer

Full Job Description
Location: Kendal/London/Manchester/UK Remote
Your responsibilities will include the following:
• The development and deployment of website pages for a varied range of clients and industries.
• Ensuring every campaign is responsive and works across different browsers and screen sizes.
• Following client brand guidelines and coding standards.
• Working alongside the design team to ensure campaigns are accurate and quality checked.
• Making amends based on in-house team members & client feedback.
• Communicating and collaborating with the design team and wider agency.
Your skills and experience:
• Strong portfolio with active website examples.
• Strong portfolio with active website examples.
• 3-5 years of commercial or agency experience in a similar role (accepting placement experience also).
• GSCE Grade B or higher in both Maths and English or equivalent.
• Computer-related bachelor’s or master’s degree.
• Knowledge and experience with front-end development - (solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and Javascript).
• Some knowledge and experience with back-end development.
• Some knowledge and experience working on CMS platforms such as Shopify, WordPress, Magento and Drupal.
• A good understanding of user-centred design principles and best practices.
• Is good with managing time and communicating timeframes.
• Outstanding listening and communication skills.
• A keen eye for detail and thrive on working on multiple varied projects.
• Has an interest in UI/UX design and development.
• An understanding of SEO approaches.
